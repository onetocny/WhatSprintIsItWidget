# Privacy Policy — WhatSprintIsItWidget

**Last updated: 14 July 2026**

WhatSprintIsItWidget ("the app", "the widget") is a Windows 11 widget that displays
the current Azure DevOps sprint and week. This policy explains what the app does and
does not do with your information.

## Summary

The app collects no data. It works entirely on your device and does not connect to
the internet.

## Information we collect

**None.** WhatSprintIsItWidget does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any
personal information or usage data. Specifically, the app:

- does **not** require an account, sign-in, or registration;
- does **not** ask for, read, or store any personal information;
- does **not** access your files, contacts, location, camera, microphone, or clipboard;
- does **not** contain advertising, analytics, tracking, or telemetry;
- does **not** contain in-app purchases.

## How the app works

The current sprint and week are calculated locally on your device from the current
date using a fixed, publicly known sprint schedule. This calculation happens entirely
offline. The app makes no network requests and sends nothing off your device.

## Data storage

The app may write small, non-personal diagnostic log files (for example, activation
status) to its own local application-data folder on your device to help with
troubleshooting. These files stay on your device, are never transmitted, and can be
deleted at any time. They contain no personal information.

## Permissions

The app uses only the standard Windows widget-provider capability required to display
a widget. It requests no additional device capabilities.

## Third parties

The app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or servers, and therefore shares
no data with any third party.

"Azure DevOps" is referenced only to describe the sprint schedule the widget tracks.
The app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Microsoft's Azure DevOps
team.

## Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same location
with a revised "Last updated" date.
